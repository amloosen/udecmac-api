You will see a small white dot in the center of the screen surrounded by a big outer white ring, as well as an orange bucket attached to the ring.

You may think of it as a white star that emits asteroids and your goal is to catch them with your bucket.

Before each time the asteroid flies out, you can choose a new location for your bucket.

So, you need to predict where the next asteroid piece will land. If the asteroid hits your orange bucket, you win points. But if it's out, you lose points.

Usually the asteroid lands in about the same location, but sometimes this will change!
